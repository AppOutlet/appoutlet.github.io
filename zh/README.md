## 🌐 Who We Are

**AppOutlet** is a small digital product studio from Braga, Portugal — a two-person partnership between **Messias Junior** (developer) and **Sarah Nogueira** (designer).

We started by building and launching our own apps, gaining firsthand experience across every stage — from concept and interface design to coding, testing, and release. Today, we use that expertise to help other projects reach their full potential.

**By integrating design and development from day one, we ensure nothing gets lost in translation.**

## 🚀 What We Do

### 📱 App Development (Multiplatform)

Custom mobile and desktop applications built with cutting-edge technologies for optimal performance and scalability.

- Consistent, premium experiences across **mobile, desktop, and web**
- Shared Kotlin Multiplatform codebase for faster delivery
- Platform-native UI designed for every device

### 🎨 UI/UX Design

User-centered design solutions that combine aesthetics with functionality to create intuitive experiences.

- **UX Strategy** — research-driven decisions, not guesswork
- **Visual Identity** — clean, scalable, consistent
- **Design Systems** — built with the build in mind, so handoff actually works

### 📈 App Store Marketing (ASO)

Strategic app store optimization and marketing campaigns to maximize visibility and downloads.

- Keyword optimization & conversion rate optimization
- Custom screenshot designs and compelling store listings
- Continuous performance tracking and iteration

### 📣 Social Media & Advertising

Professional social media management focused on driving growth and maximizing digital presence.

- Targeted advertising across Meta, Google, and LinkedIn
- Strategic content planning and community building
- Data-driven optimization with full transparency

### 🔍 Usability Testing

Comprehensive testing methodologies to ensure your app delivers the best possible user experience.

- Deep UX audits — **we don't guess, we audit**
- Severity-ranked findings with actionable recommendations
- Systematic, structured methodology

### 🌐 Web Design

Clear and functional websites that strengthen your business and make it easier for customers to find you.
