Saying Goodbye to AppOutlet: A New Chapter Begins

By messiaslima
October 25, 2023

We began our journey with a vision - to provide a platform that simplifies app discovery, making it easier for users to find and enjoy the software they love. AppOutlet was our labor of love, and it has been our pleasure to have you, our incredible community, along for the ride. Today, with mixed emotions, we have an important announcement to share.

After careful consideration, we regret to inform you that we have made the challenging decision to discontinue the AppOutlet application. This wasn't an easy choice, and it's one we made with a heavy heart. We have cherished the support and enthusiasm you've shown for AppOutlet, and your feedback has been invaluable.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to each and every one of you. Your belief in our project, your passion for finding great software, and your dedication to the AppOutlet community have been our driving force. Your support has meant the world to us, and it's what makes this announcement so bittersweet.

While we say goodbye to the AppOutlet application, we want to make it clear that the AppOutlet organization is not bidding farewell. We remain committed to our mission of creating innovative software solutions that enhance the digital experience.

We're excited about the new opportunities that lie ahead. As we step into this next chapter, we will continue to develop and deliver software solutions that are engaging, user-friendly, and tailored to your needs. We're dedicated to building products that make a positive impact, and we can't wait to share our future projects with you.

We invite you to stay connected with us. Please visit our website, where you'll find updates about our latest projects and endeavors. We value your ongoing support and look forward to sharing new, exciting developments with you. Together, we'll continue to explore the ever-evolving world of software and technology.

In parting, we'd like to express our deepest gratitude to you - our users, our supporters, and our community. The journey doesn't end here; it evolves, and we're thrilled to have you with us on this new adventure.

Thank you for being a part of the AppOutlet family. Farewell, but not goodbye.

Sincerely,

Messias Junior
Project lead @ AppOutlet
