☕️  Now you can buy me a coffee 

By messiaslima
March 2, 2022

Aiming to make things easier and provide another way to the users support the App Outlet development, we created an account on Ko-fi!

We will use the Ko-fi platform to receive donations but also to share smaller and quick updates with the community. We will post the official anouncements here on this site.

What happens with the other sponsoring platforms?

Before creating this new account, we had an Patreon and an OpenCollective account. We decided to close it because we think that it does not fit our need like Ko-fi does.

Feel free to give us some feedback! I would love to hear what you are thinking about it!

Thank you for the support that you already give to us!
